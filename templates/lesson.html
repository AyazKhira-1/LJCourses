01. Introduction to UX Design

Last updated Oct 2023

About this lesson

In this introductory lesson, we will explore the fundamental concepts of User Experience (UX) Design. You will learn what UX actually means, distinguishing it from UI (User Interface) design, and understand why it plays a crucial role in product development today.

We'll cover the core pillars of UX: Usefulness, Usability, Desirability, and Accessibility. By the end of this video, you will have a clear mental model of the UX landscape and be ready to dive into the design thinking process.
